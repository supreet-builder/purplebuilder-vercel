Best Practices

How to build a great persona

Follow these steps to create AI personas that give you feedback you can actually act on.

1
🎯
Define your audience
Pick an age range, industry and job title that closely matches your real target user.
2
🧩
Set goals & frustrations
What are they trying to achieve? What blocks them daily? The more specific, the better.
3
🤖
Use AI to fill gaps
Click "Complete with AI" on any empty field — AI will enrich your persona with realistic detail.
4
💾
Save your persona
Hit "Save Persona" and it appears in the simulation panel, ready to use immediately.
5
🚀
Start simulating
Select your persona, choose what to test, and run a simulation for deep persona-driven feedback.

Pro tips

🔍
Be hyper-specific
"35-year-old SaaS marketer in London" beats "marketing professional." Specificity drives more accurate, actionable feedback.
👥
Create 2–3 contrasting personas
Test through a tech-savvy early adopter and a cautious late adopter to catch weak spots in your content.
📋
Ground personas in real data
Pull frustrations from real customer reviews, support tickets or sales call notes for maximum realism.

Navigation Intelligence

7 behavioural signals per session

Know exactly where your users look first, what they skip, how deep they scroll, and how many times they revisit an element before deciding.

👁️
Eye-Priority Order

AI ranks every DOM element by visual attention — based on viewport position, element size, text weight, colour contrast and persona intent relevance.

📍
Cursor Coordinates

Every simulation cursor movement logs X/Y position, velocity, hover duration and direction change — a full heatmap of persona attention.

🔢
DOM Entry Sequence

Records the exact order in which elements are entered — revealing whether users follow your intended reading flow or jump around.

⏭️
Skipped Elements

Detects which sections your persona completely ignores — often the most valuable signal for layout and copy improvements.

⏱️
Time Spent per Element

Timestamped dwell data for every DOM element. See which sections hold attention and which lose it in under two seconds.

🔁
Re-entry Count

Tracks how many times the persona revisits a specific element — a strong signal of confusion, indecision or deep interest.

📜
Scroll Depth Percentage

Records the lowest DOM element reached, showing exactly how far down the page your persona scrolled before dropping off or converting.
